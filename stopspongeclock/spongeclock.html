Emergency Alert System

Immediate Warning: SpongeClock Threat

An unknown entity referred to as SpongeClock is currently posing a severe threat. This site serves as an emergency guidance to protect against SCP-1808-induced harm.

What is SpongeClock?

SpongeClock is a yellow plastic wristwatch with green polka dots and images of SpongeBob SquarePants. Despite its innocent appearance, it causes extreme psychological and physical harm through anomalous bone growth in wearers.

Protection Measures

DO NOT wear any watch resembling the described item. If encountered, isolate in complete darkness and ensure you have sufficient supplies. Report sightings immediately.

For more information or to report sightings, visit www.stopspongeclock.com
